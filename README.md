# Boston Consulting Group (BCG) — Strategy Consulting Job Simulations

This repository houses the comprehensive deliverables, analytical models, and strategic frameworks developed across two virtual job simulations completed via Forage: **Introduction to Strategy Consulting** and **Strategy Consulting**. 

The program involved executing **9 practical tasks** focused on creative problem-solving, data-driven analysis, and delivering structured, executive-ready recommendations.

---

## 🛠️ Core Approach & Frameworks

### 1. Paradigm-Busting & Creative Ideation
* **Framework Applied:** BCG’s *“Building New Boxes”* Framework[cite: 3, 7].
* **Macro Innovation:** Evaluated major global megatrends (e.g., aging demographics, urban congestion, data privacy), turning traditional industry threats into high-value market opportunities.
* **Retail Transformation (*ClothingCo Case Study*):** Dissolved rigid, season-dependent corporate assumptions for a luxury brand[cite: 7]. Shifted the business model away from traditional winter-only sales toward a year-round **"Travel & Lifestyle"** and **"Fashion Investment"** framework targeting micro-climate commuters and premium travelers[cite: 7].

### 2. Analytical & Financial Rigor
* **Financial Impact Modeling:** Benchmarked historical competitor datasets to project cash flow and profit trajectories[cite: 6]. Analyzed the short-term operational strains of distributed accounts receivable against long-term compounding interest flywheels[cite: 6].
* **Primary Market Research:** Designed a comprehensive 14-question primary tech financing preferences survey[cite: 5]. Utilized forced trade-offs (e.g., upfront down payments vs. ongoing monthly interest) to uncover explicit consumer purchasing friction points[cite: 5].

---

## 📈 Strategic Results & Deliverables

* **C-Suite Stakeholder Mapping:** Created an alignment matrix balancing competing goals across the executive suite (Sales, CFO, CTO, CMO, COO)[cite: 4]. Addressed specific technical system capabilities, up to 40% credit default rates, and brand dilution concerns[cite: 4].
* **Executive Summary Synthesis:** Packaged dense financial models, qualitative insights, and primary market research into high-impact, audience-customized strategic briefs tailored for senior leadership[cite: 3].
